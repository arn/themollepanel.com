Featured Configuration

The Hunter

The MOLLE Panel is a very adept hunters companion. Slung over the shoulder, the Panel makes a comfortable counter weight to your rifle during the walk to the hunting site. Standard MOLLE accessories create ready storage for generous amounts of field gear - GPS, water, field dressing supplies, snacks, hand warmers, and more. The Panel easily accommodates the seasons, remaining comfortable over a thin shirt or thick insulating layers. And whether it's time to dress game or perch in a stand, the easy on and off of a single sling is always welcome.
