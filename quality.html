The Berry Amendment

The Berry Amendment is a part of United States law that dictates how government agencies, such as the Department of Defense, can buy goods. Specifically, the Berry Amendment requires that preference be given to suppliers of goods that source and assemble their goods exclusively or primarily within the United States.

Learn more about the Berry Amendment on Wikipedia →

The MOLLE Panel meets the standards of the Berry Amendment. The Panel is manufactured in America at a military-grade facility. Only materials sourced from American companies are used in The Panel.
